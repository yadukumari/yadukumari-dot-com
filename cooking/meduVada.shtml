Simple Medu Vada Recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • salt to taste
  • water as required
  • oil for deep fry

Spices:

  • 1/4 tsp pepper corn crushed
  • 2 to 3 green chillies
  • chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:


  1. Wash dal and soak it with lot of water for at least 5 to 6 hrs.
  2. Add it to a mixer grinder jar , sprinkle very little water salt and start grinding.
  3. remove the paste from jar and add pepper corn, chopped green chilli, chopped coriander leaves and mix it well.
  4. mix it for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a deep heavy bottom kadai/pan till hot enough. You can drop a small amount of batter to check if the oil is hot.
  6. Dip your fingure in water, take small portion of batter shape to a ball ,slightly flatten with thumb, dip the thumb in water, make a whole in the centre, and drop in the hot oil by gently shaking off the fingers.
  7. when the oil becomes hot, put on the flame to medum.
  8. when your vada start to turn golden remove from oil and keep it in a tissue paper
  9. Do this same procedure till you get ride of paste
