Sufganiyot Recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):

  • 4 cups self-rising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 containers yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
  • 3 quarts vegitable oil for frying

Instructions:


  1. Combine flour,eggs,yogurt,sugar,salt and vanilla sugar in a larg mixing bowl,mix well. Set the dough aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Form the dough into balls with a 2- inch diameter.
  3. Heat the vegitable oil to 365 degreed F(190 degrees C)in a large pot or deep fryer over hih heat. It is best to use a basket or slotted spoon for deep frying the sufganiyot, as the oil will beextremely hot. Deep fry thee dough in the oil.Let the sufganiyot cool and drain on paper towels.
