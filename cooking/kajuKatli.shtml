Milk Burfi Simple Recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):

  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • fewsilver leaves(optional)
  • greased plate to set the barfi

Instructions:


  1. Blend cashews and milk in a blender to a fine paste.
  2. Mix paste and sgar and cook over low heat stirring till the sugar dissolves, then bring to a bol.
  3. Continue stirring over medium heat, till the mixture leaves the sides of the pan, and becomes a dough like paste.
  4. Remove from heat and when cool enough to handle, roll it on to a greased surface, with a greased rolling pin.
  5. Roll to 1/4cm or 1/8" thickness.
  6. Now cover with the silver leaf and leave to cool,thn cut into diamond shaped pieces.
