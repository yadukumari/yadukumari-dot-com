Indan Style Vegetable kurma recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):

for dough:

  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • luckwarm water
  • 2-3 garlic
  • coriander leavs
  • ghee
  • sesame seeds

How to make garlic naan recipe:-

In a small bowl add yeast, suger, salt and luckwarm water mix it add close it and keep it aside

In another bowl add white flour and yogurt mix it well.

add the yeast past and mix it well little by little till it get dough consistency

This is how the dough looks like.

Apply some oil on this dough and keep it for 1 hour.

This is how the dough raised after one hour

In a small bowl take garlic, ghee,corinder leavs and mix well.

spread the dough andd apply the garlic paste on it, and sesame seeds like below.Then place it into a tray to bake in oven.

After 15 minutes remove the tray from oven. This is how it looks.
