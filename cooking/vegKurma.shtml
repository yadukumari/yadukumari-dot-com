Indan Style Vegetable kurma recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):



for ground paste:

  • 3 tablespoon fresh coconut
  • 2 tablespoon chopped onion
  • 3-4 garlic
  • 3 inch ginger
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 3/4 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon garam masala
  • fresh coriander leavs
  • 1/2 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tomato

vegetables for kurma:

  • 1 cup of cauliflower
  • 3/4 cup of potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • 1/4 cup of fresh beans
  • 1/2 cup of carrots
  • 1/4 cup paneer

ingredients for main thadka:

  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 anise star
  • 1/2 tablespoon geera
  • bay leaf
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 cardamom
  • 1 small size onion
  • 1/2 tomato

How to make vegetable korma recipe:-


Take a pan add one tablespoon oil,onion,ginger,garlic,green chilli fry it till it get cooked.

Once it get cooked add coconute,poppy seeds,sesame seeds mix it and fry for 2-3 minutes then add coriander leavs,and let it cool down

Once it get cool down add it into a mixer jar then add tomato,garam masala,coriander powder then make a fine paste

This is how the paste looks like.

Keep a cooker on a stove and add oil then fry a paneer till get light brown

Once the paneer get fried take it outside and keep it aside.For the same oil add a ghee once it get heated add anise star,geera,bay leaf,cinnamon stick,cardamon fry it 5 secounds and then add onion and 1/2 tablespoon salt fry it till the onion cooked.

Once the onion cooked add all the vegetables and fry it for a while till it get half cook.

add a paste once the vegetables cooked and mix it well and fry for while

once the paste is get fry add yogurt and tomato and cook it till the tomato get soft.

add a water as required to cook vegetable and salt accrding to taste.

close the cooker and let it cook for two whisel or 15 minutes.

This is how it looks after two whisle. It's tasty and simple.You can have it with chapathi,naan or puri.
