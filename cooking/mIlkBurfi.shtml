Milk Burfi Simple Recipe

Ingredients(1 cup):

  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • saffron strands dry fruits or nuts(your choice)

Instructions:


  1. Butter a tray set aside
  2. In a non stick pan melt a butter on low flame.
  3. Add milk and milk powder and mix thoroughly.
  4. Then add sugar and keep stirring continuously.
  5. Cook till it becomes a lump in the middle and butter starts leaving the sides of the mixture.
  6. Transfer this mixture to the buttered tray or plate and flattern in with the back of spoon.
  7. Allow it to cool and set. Once it has cool down cut into desired shape and garnish with the soakes saffron and nuts.
