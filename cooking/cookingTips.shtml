Cooking Tips:-

Milk Shake:-
You can prepare any friut milk shake at home using fresh fruits.Try to avoid artificial fruit flavour.You can replace sugar with jaggery.Add yogurt to get resturant style thickness.
Biryani:-
After it is cooked before serve add some ghee and mix it and serve
Paysa/keer:-
After it is cooked add koya/dry fruit powder to get that thickness and it gives nice taste.
Dal:-
While cook any dal add pinch of turmeric powder and 1 tablespoon oil
